Popular Artists

Chef wa Eastlando

Chef wa Eastlando is a vibrant artist known for his energetic performances and catchy Sheng hits. Rising to fame through TikTok, his music blends traditional Sheng with modern beats, making him a favorite among Kenya's youth. His songs reflect the dynamic street culture and urban slang, resonating deeply with fans who appreciate his unique style and lyrical creativity.

Kina Njeri

Kina Njeri is a renowned Sheng artist whose music captures the essence of Nairobi's hustle. With a distinctive voice and innovative sound, Kina Njeri has gained a massive following. Her lyrics often delve into social issues and everyday experiences, providing a raw and authentic representation of urban life.
