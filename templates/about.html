About Sheng Translator

Sheng Translator was born from a deep love for Kenyan street culture and the desire to bridge the communication gap across generations. Inspired by the vibrant mix of Swahili, English, and local dialects that make up Sheng, I wanted to preserve and share this evolving language with the world.

Growing up, I watched Sheng develop into more than just street slang—it became a cultural movement. I felt it was important to document and translate Sheng so that it could be accessible to anyone, from language enthusiasts to tourists interested in this unique urban language.

**Sheng Translator** is a portfolio project for Holberton School, designed to allow users to learn, translate, and even contribute to the growth of Sheng. And the best part? It's open-source! Join us on this exciting journey to document and grow the Sheng language together.

Join the Team
